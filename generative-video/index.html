Birds of Paradise

ByCloud 2021 Generative Video Competition — Winner

Bird_Bali · 2021 · Disco Diffusion

Winner of the ByCloud 2021 Generative Video Competition. Source footage was a hand performing butterfly knife flips, fed frame-by-frame into Disco Diffusion — an early open-source diffusion model distributed as a shared Google Colab notebook, before dedicated interfaces existed — with prompts directing it toward birds of paradise. The model re-renders each frame independently, so the motion that remains is wholly the knife’s; everything else dissolves into plumage.

Disco Diffusion predates Stable Diffusion and DALL·E by about a year. Running it meant queuing shared GPU time in a Colab session and editing the prompt directly in the notebook. The competition was one of the first to recognize work made this way.

Finn Mulligan · New York
