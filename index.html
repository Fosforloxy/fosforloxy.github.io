    Borgle

    A Boggle implementation with a simulated annealing board optimizer. A random board yields around 200 findable words; hill climbing pushes that to 2,000 — making the game dramatically more interesting to play. A full solver shows everything you missed.

    An N-state cyclic cellular automaton in 3D. States eat each other in a cycle — adjust the number of states, neighborhood size, and threshold to watch the system tip between stasis and spiral chaos.
